three british citizens arrested in the united arab emirates after they were found plane spotting near fujairah airport are to be released monday , their lawyer , nasser al - hashem , tells cnn

the third man , neil munro , is a british national who lives in the uae
as a hobby , plane spotters view and photograph aircraft around the world .
